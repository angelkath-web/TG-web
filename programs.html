Pre-KG & KG – Structured Early Learning

Our early years programme focuses on developing foundational skills through structured play, guided learning, and interactive activities that nurture curiosity and confidence in young learners.

  • Early literacy and phonics development
  • Introduction to numbers and counting
  • Creative arts and expression
  • Social interaction and cooperation

Grades 1–9 – Academic Foundations

Students follow the NCERT curriculum with alignment to ICSE and Cambridge guidelines, ensuring strong subject knowledge and conceptual clarity.

  • Core academic subjects
  • Concept-based learning
  • Skill development and assessments
  • Progressive academic growth
  • Preparation for higher studies

Grades 10–12 – NIOS Senior Secondary

Senior students follow the NIOS curriculum, a recognized board under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, offering flexibility while maintaining academic standards.

  • NIOS board curriculum
  • Structured exam preparation
  • Flexible learning approach
  • Recognized certification
  • Preparation for future pathways
