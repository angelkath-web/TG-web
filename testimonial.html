Frequently Asked Questions

What curriculum do you follow?

Our learning program follows the NCERT framework with alignment to ICSE and Cambridge learning standards.

Do you offer NIOS for Grades 10–12?

Yes, we provide guidance for the NIOS Senior Secondary programme which is government recognized.

What is the class size?

We maintain small learning groups to ensure individual attention for every student.
